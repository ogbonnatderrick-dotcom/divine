    Quick Security Tips

    Simple actions that improve digital safety.

    🔒 Use Strong Passwords

    Create unique passwords with a mix of symbols, numbers, and uppercase letters.

    📱 Enable 2FA

    Add an extra layer of protection to accounts using two-factor authentication.

    ⚠ Avoid Suspicious Links

    Never click unknown links or download files from untrusted sources.

    💾 Backup Your Data

    Regular backups help protect important information from ransomware attacks.
